Basic Capabilities and Bonus Features

Basic functionalities include:

  1. new user registration
  2. login/logout for existing users
  3. new bank account creation
  4. 3 types of bank accounts (savings, checking, and credit)
  5. up to 6 different for one user
  6. joint accounts and account sharing (maximum of 4 users per account)
  7. ability to close bank accounts account
  8. account balances can be viewed for free
  9. no charge for making deposits, withdraws, or transfers between FakeBank accounts

Additional Bonus Features

  1. email verification
  2. encrypted passwords
  3. user activity logging
