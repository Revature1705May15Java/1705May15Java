Hello, welcome to A+ Bank's site navigation guide!

Here you'll learn all about the inner workings of A+ Bank's exceptional banking technology.

Account creation and logging in

As a new user, when you are welcomed you may either log in or create a new account. If you are a new user, after account creation you'll be prompted to log in.

Logging in/out

Process for logging in and out is intuitive. Enter your username and password to log in. When you wish to exit all you have to do is enter '0' to log out.

Bank Account Selection

When you log into your user account you may have multiple bank accounts open with us, you may have a maximum of 6 accounts (including those which you have jointly). Select an account to manipulate before moving on to the account navigation page. If you only have 1 active bank account open with us, you will skip this step!

Swapping Active Accounts

If you need to manipulate your other bank accounts there is no need to log out, simply select the "Switch Active Account" option by entering '6' and you'll be brought back to the menu to select which account you would like to manipulate.

Withdrawing/Depositing Money

Once you have logged in and selected an active account you will be greeted with the account navigation page. From here you many see your balance, account type, and other actions such as withdraw or deposit money from your current account.

Transferring Money

If the need arises to transfer money to a different account you may do this from the account navigation page as well. Log into the account you wish to transfer money from and enter the account number of the destination account you wish to transfer the money into.

Altering Log-in Details

Don't like your current username? Perhaps you have a better password you'd like to use. Maybe you have gotten a name change? Fret not, login to any of your bank accounts and you can select "Edit Account" by entering '3'. You will then be taken to the user account editing menu. From here, you can edit your log-in username, password, and the name on the account!

Joint Accounts

If you would like to add a secondary user to your account it's easier than ever with our new and improved joint account option in the account manipulation navigation menu. Select "Add User" by entering '4' and the other user will be prompted to log in for verification

Closing an Account

We know you'd never want to leave us. But if someone forces you (or perhaps you simply need to condense two checking accounts?) we have the solution for you. Closing an account is simple, you will need to withdraw all remaining funds before closing the account and if possible, please leave us a comment so we can improve in the future.
